Sop Ayam

Rich Umami Soup

Sop Ayam is a extra rich chicken broth soup

  • Chicken
  • Longbean
  • Garlic
  • Shallots
  • Salt and Pepper

follow the instruction below to cook the Sop Ayam

  1. Stir fry your garlic
  2. Add water
  3. Wait until boiling before adding chicken
  4. After chicken's done boiling, add the longbeans
  5. Finish and serve

and that's it! you can serve it with hot rice
